Build a Wall-Plastering Robot

Do you like robotics and programming and want to build something that actually has real-world impact? A construction company in Portugal is exploring automation for wall plastering, a critical and labor-intensive process in construction. I'm putting together a small, committed team to design and build a wall-plastering robot to address this problem.

This project combines robotics, mechanical and software engineering, and working with real construction materials onsite. We'll be doing hands-on prototyping, testing, breaking things, and iterating until it works.

Timeline (Now → End of March)

From now through the end of March, the team will focus exclusively on producing a high-quality technical proposal, including:

  • System architecture
  • Design process
  • Cost estimates
  • Risk analysis
  • Implementation roadmap

Physical prototyping and building expected to begin after April.

Commitment & Expectations

This is a volunteer project—there is no compensation, but all project expenses will be covered. In return, full commitment is expected. The goal is to deliver a fully functional product.

This project is expected to be long-term, extending beyond the initial proposal phase.

Backgrounds That Fit Well

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Robotics / Controls
  • Computer Science (especially hardware-adjacent work)
  • Construction, drywall, or plastering experience

Roles Needed

  • Mechanical / Manufacturing (1–2 people)
  • Controls / Software (1–2 people)
  • Materials / Construction Advisor (0–1 person)
