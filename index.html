Build a Wall-Plastering Robot

Do you like robotics and programming and want to build something that actually has a real-world impact? A construction company in Portugal is exploring automation for wall plastering (read more), a critical and labor intensive process in construction. I'm putting together a small, committed team to design and build a wall-plastering robot to address this problem for them.

This project combines robotics, mechanical, software engineering and working with real construction materials onsite. We'll do hands-on prototyping and testing, break things, and go through rigorous iterations.

Timeline (Now → End of March)

From now through the end of March, the team will focus exclusively on producing a detailed technical proposal, including:

  • Team structure
  • System architecture
  • Design process
  • Cost estimates
  • Risk analysis
  • Implementation roadmap

Physical prototyping and building are expected to begin after April.

Commitment & Expectations

This is a voluntary/interest-based project. In other words, there is no compensation. However, there will also be no cost at any given time. All project expenses will be covered by an investor. In return, full commitment is expected. The goal is to eventually deliver a fully functional product.

This project is expected to be long-term, extending beyond the initial proposal phase to span multiple quarters.

Backgrounds That Fit Well

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Robotics / Controls
  • Computer Science (especially hardware-adjacent)
  • Construction, drywall, or plastering experience

Roles Needed

  • Mechanical / Manufacturing (1–2 people)
  • Controls / Software (1–2 people)
  • Materials / Construction Advisor (0–1 person)

About Me
